Help Center

How do I create a Secret Coder account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive instructions on resetting your password via the email associated with your account.

How do I enroll in a course?

Navigate to the "Courses" section, choose a course, and click on the "Enroll" button. Follow the prompts to start the course

Can I switch between courses?

Yes, you can switch between courses at any time. Your progress in each course will be saved.

I'm experiencing technical issues with the interactive coding feature. What should I do?

Clear your browser cache, update your browser to the latest version, and ensure that JavaScript is enabled. If issues persist, contact our technical support team.

The videos are not playing. How can I fix this?

Make sure your internet connection is stable. If the issue persists, try accessing the content in a different browser or device. Contact support if the problem continues.

How can I join the Secret Coder community?

Click on the "Community" link in the navigation menu to access our forums. Create an account if you haven't already, and start engaging with the community.

Can I ask questions in the forums

Absolutely! Feel free to ask questions, share insights, and participate in coding discussions within our community forums.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, Secret Coder employs industry-standard encryption to ensure the security of your payment information. Your data is handled with the utmost care.
